Chicken Curry

Description

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 500g chicken, cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Saut√© onions until translucent.
  3. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Stir in curry powder and cook for another minute.
  5. Add chicken and cook until browned.
  6. Pour in coconut milk, stir, and bring to a simmer.
  7. Season with salt and pepper.
  8. Simmer until chicken is cooked through.
